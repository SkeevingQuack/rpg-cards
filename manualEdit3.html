Ring of the Daedra

Attunement

Required

If you plunge the hand which bears this ring into the core of a recently dead creature, you regain the use of a spell slot less than or equal to half of its CR. If you attempt to do the same to a living creature, make an unarmed melee attack roll with disadvantage. If your attack kills the target, regain two spell slots instead of one.

Unarmed attacks with this hand have proficiency, and the damage die starts out as 1d4. Every time you absorb energy, add one point of corruption if the target was dead or two if the target was alive. Corruption increases the damage die, and resets only if the ring is removed.
