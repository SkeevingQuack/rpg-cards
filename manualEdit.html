The first time an attack with this weapon hits, it does an additional 1 fire damage. This damage doubles every successful hit after the first (max 16), but the damage resets any time an attack misses or a round passes without it hitting anything.

For every round the Shard is left embedded, treat it as two consecutive successful attacks (without piercing damage). It can only be embedded in an unwilling creature with a critical hit, and it can be removed by that creature as if drawing a weapon.
