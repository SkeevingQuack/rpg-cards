Magma Shard

Attunement

Required

Weapon

melee, finesse, light
+0, 1d6 piercing + ~

Proficiency

martial (shortsword)

The first time an attack with this weapon hits, it does an additional 1 fire damage. This damage doubles every successful hit after the first (max 16), but the damage resets any time an attack misses or a round passes without it hitting anything.

For every round the Shard is left embedded, treat it as two consecutive successful attacks (without piercing damage). It can only be embedded in an unwilling creature with a critical hit, and it can be removed by that creature as if drawing a weapon.

Attunement

None

Seems to allow a caster to maintain concentration on an additional transmutation spell. In order to gain this benefit the spike needs to be driven into the base of the skull, though the sepcifics of this procedure are murky. Outside of its host, the Spike seems to be losing its magic over time.

Court Dagger

Attunement

None

Weapon

melee, finesse, light
+2, 1d4+2 piercing

Proficiency

simple (dagger)

Attacks with this weapon have the critical range increased by 1. If you take a minute to hide this dagger on your person, it takes on the tone of your skin, clings to your skin until intentionally removed (by anyone), and bends and shifts with your body so it never cuts or discomforts you.

Gloves of Missile Snar

Attunement

Required

When a ranged weapon attack hits you while you're wearing these gloves, you can use your reaction to reduce the damage by 1d10 + your Dexterity modifier, provided that you have a free hand. If you reduce the damage to 0, you can catch the missile if it is small enough for you to hold in that hand.

These gloves are a deep violet covered in web of silver filigree. As you attune to them, they seem to almost meld into your flesh.
