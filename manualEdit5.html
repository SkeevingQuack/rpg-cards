Amulet of Mighty Fists

Attunement

Required

You can make unarmed attacks as if they are natural weapons with damage 1d4. All natural weapon attacks you make count as magical and have a +1 bonus to attack and damage.

A necklace of large chain links made of ivory, each link carved with delicate images of claws, fists, feet, and fangs.

Quiver of Ehlonna

Attunement

None

Each of the quiver's three compartments connects to an extradimensional space that allows the quiver to hold numerous items while never weighing more than 2 pounds. The shortest compartment can hold up to sixty arrows, bolts or similar objects. The midsize compartment holds up to eighteen javelins or similar objects. The longest compartment holds up to six long objects such as bows, quarterstaffs, or spears.

You can draw any item the quiver contains as if doing so from a regular quiver or scabbard.

Arrow of Fiend Slaying

Attunement

None

A fiend hit with this arrow takes an extra 6d10 typeless damage (DC 17 Constitution save to reduce damage by half).

Unlike most weapon enchantments, slaying arrows are even more fragile than normal arrows and have a 25% chance to break on a miss (subject to change based on circumstance).

Runechill Hatchet

Attunement

Required

Weapon

melee, versatile (1d10)
+2, 1d8+2 slashing

Proficiency

martial (battleaxe)

Once per long rest as a free action, you may cause the runes on the blade to flare up with flickering cold blue light for 5 rounds. Creatures hit while this is active take an extra 1d6 necrotic damage. Living cratures hit with this must make a DC 12 Strength save or have disadvantage on all attack rolls until a short or long rest. Undead creatures must make a DC 12 Charisma save or become frightened and attempt to flee until a short or long rest.

Revelation Quill

Attunement

None

If placed in an empty vial or other glass container of similar size and left there for an hour, the container is filled with ink. The ink is of random color 50% of the time, otherwise it is black.

Once per day, if the user concentrates on a speciic future goal, event, or activity occurring within the coming week, this quill takes over and writes out a short phrase in response, often in the form of a cryptic rhyme or omen, much as divination.

Once per week, the user may ask up to 10 questions of the mysterious intellect that guides this quill.

Robe of Runes

Attunement

Required

Raise your Intelligence score (and score maximum) by 2. Once per long rest as a free action, you can regain 4 levels of spell slots. For 1 round after doing so, your spell attack and spell save DC increases by 1.

Pearl of Limited Wish

Attunement

None

As you crush this pearl, you may duplicate any wizard spell of 6th level or lower or any spell of 5th level or lower. It is cast as if you had a +4 proficiency bonus and 22 in the spellcasting ability score.
