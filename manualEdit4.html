Burlap Sack of Holding

Attunement

None

This bag has an interior space considerably larger than its outside dimensions, roughly 2 feet in diameter at the mouth and 4 feet deep. The bag can hold up to 500 pounds and its inside is a 4-foot cube, but the bag can always be folded as if empty. Retrieving an item from the bag requires at least an action, depending on your organizational ability.

Bag of Holding

Attunement

None

This bag has an interior space considerably larger than the trendy coinpurse it appears to be. The bag can hold up to 500 pounds, not exceeding a volume of 64 cubic feet.

Placing an item in the bag of holding requires a bonus action in which you place the item inside the bag of holding and speak a name. Removing an item from the bag requires an action in which you place your hand inside the bag and speak the name of the item. The bag's mouth can expand to 2 feet in diameter in order to swallow something.

Winged Boots

Attunement

Required

While you wear these boots, you have a flying speed equal to your walking speed. You can use the boots to fly for up to 4 hours, all at once or in several shorter flights, each one using a minimum of 1 minute from the duration. If you are flying when the duration expires, you descend at a rate of 30 feet per round until you land.

The boots regain 2 hours of flying capability every day at dawn

Wand of Witch Bolt

Attunement

Required

This wand has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast the Witch Bolt spell from it. For each charge beyond the first, increase the level of the spell by 1. It regains 2d4 charges daily at dawn. If the wand contains 0 charges and you roll an 8 on its recharge, the wand shatters and deals 1d12 lightning damage to anyone holding it at the time.

Manual of Golems

Attunement

None

This tome contains the incantations necessary to imbue a golem with life. In order to use it, you must be a spellcaster with at least 2 5th-level spell slots. In order to use it, a page is torn out and placed inside the mouth of the golem. Only a limited number of pages remain.

The basic prerequisites for a valid golem body are explained, but no other construction guidance is given.

Griffon Saddle

Attunement

Required

You gain the senses of anyone who rides you atop this saddle. The increased awareness gives attacks against you disadvantage and ensures that your rider is never dismounted unless you wish them to be.

Only functions while the rider is conscious.

Sending Stones

Attunement

None

Sending Stones come in pairs, with each smooth stone carved to match the other so the pairing is easily recognized. While you touch one stone, you can use an action to cast the sending spell from it. The target is the bearer of the other stone. If no creature bears the other stone, you know that fact as soon as you use the stone and don't cast the spell.

If one of the stones is destroyed, the other one becomes nonmagical.

Aneara and Aljaan have these. At some point I'll figure out a better system, but I'm not printing another page for one more card.

Sending Darwin

Attunement

None

You can use this to cast the sending spell targeting Darwin. This has worked every time you've tried it, but you aren't sure of the specifics.

A smooth, egg-shaped stone, half of which is a dark, ruddy color while the other half is transparent. A carved relief of Darwin's face protrudes into the transparent half. If you catch a glimpse of the stone out of the corner of your eye, Darwin appears to salaciously wink at you.
